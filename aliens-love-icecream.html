Melting Planets

The truth behind the sweet disaster!

Leaked files · Intergalactic Icecream Council

For centuries astronomers assumed planets melted because of volcanic activity, unstable atmospheres, or stars expanding into red giants.

Cute theory.

Recently leaked files from the Intergalactic Icecream Council suggest something much sweeter. According to anonymous dessert engineers from the Andromeda Gelato Collective, certain worlds are actually made entirely of frozen cosmic dairy suspended in plasma fields. Their stability depends on a delicate balance between moonlight, dreams, and the collective happiness of nearby civilizations.

When an intelligent species becomes overly stressed, doom-scrolls too much, or forgets how to laugh, nearby icecream planets begin to soften.

Scientists call this thermal instability. Aliens simply call it "the emotional drip."

Entire civilizations have reportedly vanished beneath rivers of rainbow sherbet after one particularly disappointing season finale.

Coincidence? We'll let you decide.

UFO Sightings

Are they after our desserts?!

Independent Cone Watch · Eyes on the skies (and the sundaes)

Government officials continue insisting unidentified flying objects pose no threat.

Interesting. Then why do UFO sightings spike near icecream trucks?

Independent Cone Watch researchers analyzed decades of eyewitness reports and discovered an unusual pattern: chocolate festivals. Summer fairs. Boardwalks. Roadside soft-serve stands.

Coincidence appears increasingly difficult to defend.

Several witnesses claim mysterious beams of pastel light selectively removed triple-scoop cones while leaving vegetables completely untouched. One child even reported hearing a tiny voice whisper:

"Extra sprinkles accepted."

The case remains deliciously unexplained.
