👑 Wonderland Weekly · Summer 2026

Alice & The Cheshire Cat

Inside the most iconic duo in Wonderland — destiny, madness & the art of getting lost.

Some celebrity friendships are built on fame. Others are built on chaos. For decades, Wonderland has whispered about the strange connection between Alice and the Cheshire Cat — the curious dreamer and the cosmic trickster whose grin has become one of the most recognizable symbols in all alternate realities.

They disappear. They reappear. They speak in riddles. They vanish before answering questions. And somehow… they always seem to know where the next portal opens.

“People think Wonderland is about nonsense,” Alice says thoughtfully. “But I think it's really about becoming someone new every time you get lost.” The Cheshire Cat grins wider. “Getting lost is the whole point.”

“We're all mad here” — but madness isn't chaos. It's what happens when you stop pretending the world makes perfect sense.
