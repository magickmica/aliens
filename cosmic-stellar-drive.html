Cosmic Archaeology Institute · Entirely fictional, delightfully so

For centuries historians insisted the Great Pyramids were tombs. But according to anonymous researchers from the fictional "Cosmic Archaeology Institute," the monuments may actually be giant planetary tuning forks designed to stabilize Earth's energetic field.
